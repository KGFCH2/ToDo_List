    How Email Reminders Work

    Email Task Reminders

    How It Works

    1. Create or edit a task and set an end date & time
    2. The system automatically schedules a reminder
    3. 15 minutes before the due time, you'll receive an email
    4. The email contains your task details and a link to TaskFlow Pro

    Example

    If your task is due at 5:00 PM today, you'll receive a reminder email at 4:45 PM
